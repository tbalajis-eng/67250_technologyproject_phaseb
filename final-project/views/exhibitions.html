Exhibition 1: Fall Offerings - Crystals in Art: Ancient to Today

This exhibition explores the numerous ways that people use crystals in artwork to make profound statements and beautiful works. Crystals have been a pillar in artwork since the begining of man and this exhibition shows both the beauty and history of these crystals.

Exhibition 2: Exquisite Creatures: Butterflies and Beatles

Using the beauty of the natural wold, this exhibition is focused on using the natural world in order to make their artworks. Whether it be butterfly spirals or beatles laid out next to each other, come see how artists use the normal everyday creatures around you to create something magical!

Exhibition 3: Outdoor Architecture — The Giant Spider

As one of the most popular installments in our Outdoor art selection, the Giant Spider represents a feat of Architecture that attracts numerous people from across the country to watch it and its childer welcome people to the great MonoMuse Museum.
